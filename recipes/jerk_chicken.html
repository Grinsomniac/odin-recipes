Jamaican Jerk Chicken

Description

This baked jerk chicken is not too sweet, not too spicy. I was on the hunt for a perfect jerk seasoning and ended up making my own blend. Hope you like it!

Ingredients

  • Salt: 1 teaspoon, or to taste
  • Ground allspice: 1 teaspoon
  • Brown sugar: 1 teaspoon, packed
  • Onion powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Dried minced garlic: ½ teaspoon
  • Ground nutmeg: ½ teaspoon
  • Black pepper: ½ teaspoon
  • Ground ginger: ½ teaspoon
  • Cayenne pepper: ¼ teaspoon
  • Ground cinnamon: ¼ teaspoon
  • Dried thyme leaves: ¼ teaspoon
  • Whole chicken: 1 (2 to 3 pound), cut into pieces
  • Vegetable oil: ¼ cup

Steps

  1. Mix all spices in a large bowl; add chicken pieces and toss to coat. Marinate in the refrigerator for 2 to 4 hours.
  2. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Pour vegetable oil into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Place chicken pieces skin-side up in the baking dish. Bake until juices run clear, about 1 hour 20 minutes. Chicken should reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  4. Turn the oven to broil, and cook until the skin crisps, about 2 to 5 minutes.
